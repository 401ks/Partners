Frequently Asked Questions

How does the security deposit work? expand_more
The security deposit (5,000-10,000 LKR) serves as commitment to service quality. It's held securely and fully refundable with 30 days notice. If a customer refuses payment despite our pre-agreed Terms of Service, the job cost is deducted from your deposit to protect you. If your balance drops to 2,500 LKR, lead delivery pauses until you top up.
What if a customer refuses to pay? expand_more
We verify every customer's intent, urgency, and budget before dispatch. Each booking includes a digital Terms of Service agreement signed by the customer. If a customer refuses payment despite this agreement, we blacklist them permanently and provide you a free replacement lead. For Pay-Per-Lead partners, unpaid jobs are deducted from your security deposit to protect your earnings.
How fast are leads delivered? expand_more
Within 2-5 minutes of our qualification call, sent directly to your WhatsApp. You must respond within 5 minutes to ACCEPT or DECLINE. Fast response = more jobs. Leads include: customer name, phone, location pin, issue type, and urgency level.
Prepaid Credits vs. Area Buyout? expand_more
Credits offer flexibility - buy what you need, when you need it. Our credits never expire, and we offer a "Credit Guarantee" for invalid leads. Area Buyouts (~20,000 LKR/mo first-run) provide exclusive rights to a specific postal zone with zero competition. Best for established businesses wanting predictable monthly costs.
How do payouts work? expand_more
We collect payments via bank transfer (card system coming soon). Funds are deposited to your registered bank account weekly (every Friday). Minimum payout threshold: 5,000 LKR. Lead costs are deducted before payout. You'll receive a WhatsApp notification when funds are sent.
